INSECTS

LADY BUG
This friendly, bright-colored beetle is found all over the world. It eats garden pests and its black spots warn birds that it is not good to eat.

BUTTERFLY
Butterflies spend the first part of their lives as caterpillars, before turning into "sleeping" chrysalis. Then they finally hatch out as adult butterflies.

GIRAFFE WEEVIL
This weevil has a very strange long neck, and we still do not know what it is used for!

MOTH
Moths are different from butterflies since they like to fly at night. They feed on the nectar in flowers and spend the first part of their lives as caterpillars

DRAGONFLY
Dragonflies come in many colors and have either one or two pairs of wings. They spend one or more years as underwayer "nymph" before hatching into an adult.
